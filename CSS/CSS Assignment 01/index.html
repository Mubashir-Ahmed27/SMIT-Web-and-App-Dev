About Allah's Mercy (Rahmah)

Imagine how the rain falls - soft, steady,
healing. It touches dry earth and brings life where everything once seemed still. A few drops, and suddenly, green returns.

Reflection

Now think about our lives. What if Allah's mercy touched us like that rain? What sorrows would ease? What joy would return?
What part of us would come back to life?
Let's never stop asking Allah for His Rahmah.
Here is a Dua of the Prophet (peace be upon him) asking for Allah's mercy.

Dua for Mercy

أَضْلِحْ عَيْنٍ، طَرْفَةَ نَفْسِي إِلَى تَكِلْنِي فَلَا أَزْجُو، رَحْمَتَكَ اللَّهُمَّ كُلَّهُ شَأْنِي لِي
O Allah, Your mercy is what I hope for, so do not leave me to myself for even the blink of an eye, and make all of my affairs well.
