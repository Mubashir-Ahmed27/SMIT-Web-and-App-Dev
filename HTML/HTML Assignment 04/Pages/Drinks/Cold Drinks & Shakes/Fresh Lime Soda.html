🍋🥤 Fresh Lime Soda

Description:
Fresh Lime Soda is a zesty, refreshing drink made with lime juice, soda, and a touch of sugar or salt. Perfect for hot days, it’s a cool, tangy thirst-quencher that’s easy to make and loved by all ages. Enjoy it sweet or salty, garnished with mint for extra freshness!

📝 Recipe Ingredients

  • 🍋 Fresh lime juice
  • 🥤 Chilled soda water
  • 🍬 Sugar (for sweet version)
  • 🧂 Salt (for salty version)
  • 🌿 Mint leaves (for garnish)
  • 🧊 Ice cubes
