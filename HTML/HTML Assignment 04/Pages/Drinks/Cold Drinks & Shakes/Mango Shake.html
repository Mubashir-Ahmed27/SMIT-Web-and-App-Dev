🥭🥤 Mango Shake

Description:
Mango Shake is a creamy, tropical delight made with ripe mangoes, milk, and a touch of sugar. Served chilled, it’s a refreshing treat that’s perfect for hot summer days and loved by all ages. Enjoy this sweet, fruity shake for a burst of sunshine in every sip!

📝 Recipe Ingredients

  • 🥭 Ripe mangoes (peeled and chopped)
  • 🥛 Chilled milk
  • 🍬 Sugar (to taste)
  • 🧊 Ice cubes
  • 🍦 Vanilla ice cream (optional, for extra creaminess)
  • 🌿 Cardamom powder (optional)
