🥤☕ Cold Coffee

Description:
Cold Coffee is a creamy, chilled beverage that blends rich coffee with milk, sugar, and ice. Perfect for hot days, it’s smooth, refreshing, and energizing—often topped with whipped cream or chocolate syrup for extra indulgence. Enjoy Cold Coffee as a cool treat any time!

📝 Recipe Ingredients

  • ☕ Instant coffee or brewed coffee
  • 🥛 Chilled milk
  • 🍬 Sugar (to taste)
  • 🧊 Ice cubes
  • 🍫 Chocolate syrup (optional)
  • 🍦 Whipped cream (optional)
