🧃 Rooh Afza

Description:
Rooh Afza is a classic South Asian summer drink, famous for its vibrant red color and refreshing floral flavor. Served chilled, it’s a sweet, cooling beverage made with herbal syrup, water, and sometimes milk or lemon. Rooh Afza is a must-have at iftar tables and festive gatherings!

📝 Recipe Ingredients

  • 🧃 Rooh Afza syrup
  • 💧 Chilled water
  • 🥛 Milk (optional, for creamy version)
  • 🍋 Lemon juice (optional, for tangy version)
  • 🧊 Ice cubes
