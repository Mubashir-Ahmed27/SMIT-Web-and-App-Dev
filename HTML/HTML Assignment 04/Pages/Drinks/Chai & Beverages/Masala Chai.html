☕ Masala Chai

Description:
Masala Chai is a beloved South Asian spiced tea, brewed with black tea leaves, milk, and a blend of warming spices. Aromatic, comforting, and energizing, it’s perfect for chilly mornings or relaxing evenings. Enjoyed with snacks or on its own, Masala Chai is a true taste of tradition!

📝 Recipe Ingredients

  • ☕ Black tea leaves
  • 🥛 Milk
  • 💧 Water
  • 🍬 Sugar (to taste)
  • 🌿 Spices: cardamom, ginger, cinnamon, cloves, black pepper
