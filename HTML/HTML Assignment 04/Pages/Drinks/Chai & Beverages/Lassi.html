🥤 Lassi (Sweet/Salty)

Description:
Lassi is a refreshing traditional yogurt-based drink, enjoyed sweet or salty across South Asia. Creamy, cooling, and perfect for hot days, it’s often flavored with sugar, salt, spices, or fruit. Lassi is a staple at meals and a delicious way to beat the heat!

📝 Recipe Ingredients

  • 🥛 Yogurt (curd)
  • 💧 Chilled water
  • 🍬 Sugar (for sweet lassi)
  • 🧂 Salt (for salty lassi)
  • 🌿 Cardamom powder or roasted cumin (optional)
  • 🍋 Lemon juice (optional, for salty lassi)
  • 🍓 Fruit (mango, strawberry, optional for flavored lassi)
