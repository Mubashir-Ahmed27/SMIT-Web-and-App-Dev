🥩🍚 Beef Biryani

Description:
Beef Biryani is a beloved Pakistani delicacy, featuring succulent beef pieces cooked with fragrant basmati rice and a medley of bold spices. This hearty dish is known for its rich flavor, vibrant color, and irresistible aroma. Perfect for gatherings and celebrations, it’s a true feast for biryani lovers!

📝 Recipe Ingredients

  • 🥩 Beef pieces (preferably with bone)
  • 🍚 Basmati rice
  • 🥛 Yogurt
  • 🧅 Onions, 🍅 tomatoes
  • 🧄 Ginger-garlic paste
  • 🌶️ Biryani masala, whole spices (bay leaf, cardamom, cloves, cinnamon)
  • 🌿 Green chilies, coriander, mint
  • 🧈 Oil/ghee, 🧂 salt, and 🍋 lemon juice
