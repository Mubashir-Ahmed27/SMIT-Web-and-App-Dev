🍗🍚 Chicken Biryani

Description:
Chicken Biryani is a classic Pakistani rice dish, famous for its aromatic spices 🌶️, tender chicken 🍗, and flavorful basmati rice 🍚. Each bite offers a perfect blend of heat, fragrance, and richness, making it a favorite for festive occasions and family meals. This dish is layered, cooked on dum, and served with raita 🥣 or salad 🥗.

📝 Recipe Ingredients

  • 🍗 Chicken pieces (bone-in or boneless)
  • 🍚 Basmati rice
  • 🥛 Yogurt
  • 🧅 Onions, 🍅 tomatoes
  • 🧄 Ginger-garlic paste
  • 🌶️ Biryani masala, whole spices (bay leaf, cardamom, cloves, cinnamon)
  • 🌿 Green chilies, coriander, mint
  • 🧈 Oil/ghee, 🧂 salt, and 🍋 lemon juice
