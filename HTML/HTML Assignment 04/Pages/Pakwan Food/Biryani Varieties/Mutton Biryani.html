🥩🍚 Mutton Biryani

Description:
Mutton Biryani is a royal delicacy, celebrated for its tender mutton pieces, fragrant basmati rice, and rich blend of spices 🌶️. Each layer is infused with flavor, making it a festive favorite and a true showstopper at gatherings. Enjoyed with raita 🥣 or salad 🥗, this biryani is a must-try for meat lovers.

📝 Recipe Ingredients

  • 🥩 Mutton pieces (bone-in preferred)
  • 🍚 Basmati rice
  • 🥛 Yogurt
  • 🧅 Onions, 🍅 tomatoes
  • 🧄 Ginger-garlic paste
  • 🌶️ Biryani masala, whole spices (bay leaf, cardamom, cloves, cinnamon)
  • 🌿 Green chilies, coriander, mint
  • 🧈 Oil/ghee, 🧂 salt, and 🍋 lemon juice
