🥘🐑 Mutton Korma

Description:
Mutton Korma is a royal Mughlai dish, celebrated for its rich, creamy gravy and tender mutton pieces. Slow-cooked with aromatic spices, yogurt, and fried onions, this delicacy is a centerpiece at festive gatherings and special occasions. Its luxurious taste and silky texture make it a true classic of Pakistani cuisine.

📝 Recipe Ingredients

  • 🐑 Mutton (bone-in pieces)
  • 🧅 Fried onions
  • 🥛 Yogurt
  • 🧄 Ginger-garlic paste
  • 🌶️ Red chili powder, turmeric, coriander powder
  • 🧈 Oil or ghee
  • 🥥 Cashew or almond paste (optional, for richness)
  • 🌿 Whole spices (cardamom, cloves, cinnamon, bay leaf)
  • 🧂 Salt
