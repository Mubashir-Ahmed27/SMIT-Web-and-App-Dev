🍗🍲 Chicken Karahi

Description:
Chicken Karahi is a beloved Pakistani dish, cooked in a wok-like pan called a karahi. It features juicy chicken pieces simmered in a spicy tomato-based gravy, infused with ginger, garlic, and green chilies. Served sizzling hot, Chicken Karahi is perfect for sharing and pairs beautifully with naan or roti.

📝 Recipe Ingredients

  • 🍗 Chicken pieces (bone-in preferred)
  • 🍅 Tomatoes
  • 🧄 Ginger-garlic paste
  • 🌶️ Green chilies
  • 🧂 Salt, red chili powder, black pepper
  • 🧈 Oil or ghee
  • 🌿 Fresh coriander, ginger (julienned) for garnish
