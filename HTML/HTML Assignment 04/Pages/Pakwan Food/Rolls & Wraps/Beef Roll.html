🌯🥩 Beef Roll

Description:
Beef Roll is a mouthwatering street food favorite, featuring spicy grilled or shredded beef wrapped in a soft paratha or flatbread. Loaded with tangy sauces, fresh veggies, and herbs, each bite delivers bold flavor and satisfying texture. Perfect for lunch, dinner, or a quick snack on the go!

📝 Recipe Ingredients

  • 🥩 Grilled or shredded beef
  • 🌯 Paratha or flatbread
  • 🥗 Sliced onions, tomatoes, lettuce
  • 🌶️ Green chilies, coriander, mint
  • 🥣 Yogurt or mayo-based sauce
  • 🧂 Salt, pepper, chaat masala
  • 🍋 Lemon juice
