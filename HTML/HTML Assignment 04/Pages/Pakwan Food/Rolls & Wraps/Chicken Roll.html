🌯🍗 Chicken Roll

Description:
Chicken Roll is a popular street food snack, featuring spicy grilled chicken wrapped in a soft paratha or flatbread. Bursting with flavor, it’s layered with tangy sauces, fresh veggies, and herbs for a satisfying bite. Perfect for lunch, dinner, or on-the-go cravings, Chicken Roll is a favorite among all ages.

📝 Recipe Ingredients

  • 🍗 Grilled or shredded chicken
  • 🌯 Paratha or flatbread
  • 🥗 Sliced onions, tomatoes, lettuce
  • 🌶️ Green chilies, coriander, mint
  • 🥣 Yogurt or mayo-based sauce
  • 🧂 Salt, pepper, chaat masala
  • 🍋 Lemon juice
