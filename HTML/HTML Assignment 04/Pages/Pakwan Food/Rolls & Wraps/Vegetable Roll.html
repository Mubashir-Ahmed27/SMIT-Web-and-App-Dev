🌯🥦 Vegetable Roll

Description:
Vegetable Roll is a delicious and healthy street food snack, packed with sautéed mixed veggies and wrapped in a soft paratha or flatbread. Bursting with flavor and color, it’s layered with tangy sauces and fresh herbs for a satisfying bite. Perfect for vegetarians and anyone craving a light, tasty treat!

📝 Recipe Ingredients

  • 🥦 Mixed vegetables (carrot, cabbage, capsicum, peas, potato)
  • 🌯 Paratha or flatbread
  • 🧅 Onion, 🍅 tomato
  • 🌶️ Green chilies, coriander, mint
  • 🥣 Yogurt or mayo-based sauce
  • 🧂 Salt, pepper, chaat masala
  • 🍋 Lemon juice
