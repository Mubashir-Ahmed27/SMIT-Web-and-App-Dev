🥓🔥 Seekh Kebabs

Description:
Seekh Kebabs are a popular grilled delicacy, made from spiced minced meat shaped onto skewers and cooked over open flames. Juicy, smoky, and bursting with flavor, these kebabs are a favorite at BBQs and street food stalls. Served hot with chutney and salad, Seekh Kebabs are a true treat for meat lovers!

📝 Recipe Ingredients

  • 🥩 Minced beef, mutton, or chicken
  • 🧅 Onion (finely chopped)
  • 🧄 Ginger-garlic paste
  • 🌶️ Green chilies, coriander, mint
  • 🧂 Salt, red chili powder, garam masala
  • 🥚 Egg (for binding)
  • 🛢️ Oil or ghee (for basting)
  • 🌿 Fresh coriander & lemon wedges (for garnish)
