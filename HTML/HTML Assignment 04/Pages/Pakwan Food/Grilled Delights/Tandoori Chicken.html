🍗🔥 Tandoori Chicken

Description:
Tandoori Chicken is a world-famous grilled delicacy, marinated in yogurt and spices, then cooked in a traditional clay oven (tandoor) for a smoky flavor and juicy texture. Its vibrant color and bold taste make it a favorite at BBQs and festive gatherings. Served hot with lemon wedges and mint chutney, it's a true showstopper!

📝 Recipe Ingredients

  • 🍗 Chicken (whole or pieces, bone-in preferred)
  • 🥛 Yogurt
  • 🧄 Ginger-garlic paste
  • 🌶️ Red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala
  • 🍋 Lemon juice
  • 🧂 Salt
  • 🛢️ Oil
  • 🌿 Fresh coriander & mint (for garnish)
