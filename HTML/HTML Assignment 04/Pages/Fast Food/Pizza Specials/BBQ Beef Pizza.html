🍕🥩 BBQ Beef Pizza

Description:
BBQ Beef Pizza is a mouthwatering fusion of smoky barbecue flavors and classic pizza goodness. Topped with tender beef, tangy BBQ sauce, melted cheese, and fresh veggies, every slice delivers a bold, satisfying taste. Perfect for parties, family dinners, or whenever you crave something truly special!

📝 Recipe Ingredients

  • 🥩 BBQ beef (grilled or shredded)
  • 🍕 Pizza dough/base
  • 🧀 Mozzarella and cheddar cheese
  • 🍅 Tomato sauce
  • 🥫 BBQ sauce
  • 🌶️ Sliced onions, bell peppers, green chilies
  • 🧂 Salt, pepper, Italian herbs
