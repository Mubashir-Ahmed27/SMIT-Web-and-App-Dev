🍕🍗 Chicken Tikka Pizza

Description:
Chicken Tikka Pizza is a fusion favorite, combining spicy, marinated chicken tikka with classic pizza flavors. Topped with mozzarella, tangy sauce, and fresh veggies, every slice delivers a burst of bold taste and irresistible aroma. Perfect for parties, family dinners, or whenever you crave something truly unique!

📝 Recipe Ingredients

  • 🍗 Chicken tikka (marinated and grilled)
  • 🍕 Pizza dough/base
  • 🧀 Mozzarella and cheddar cheese
  • 🍅 Tomato sauce
  • 🌶️ Sliced onions, bell peppers, green chilies
  • 🧂 Salt, pepper, Italian herbs
