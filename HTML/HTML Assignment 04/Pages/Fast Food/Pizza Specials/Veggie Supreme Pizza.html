🍕🥦 Veggie Supreme Pizza

Description:
Veggie Supreme Pizza is a colorful, flavor-packed delight loaded with fresh vegetables and gooey cheese. Each slice bursts with the goodness of bell peppers, mushrooms, olives, and more, making it a perfect choice for vegetarians and pizza lovers alike. Enjoy this wholesome treat at parties, family dinners, or any time you crave a veggie feast!

📝 Recipe Ingredients

  • 🍕 Pizza dough/base
  • 🧀 Mozzarella and cheddar cheese
  • 🌶️ Bell peppers (red, green, yellow)
  • 🍄 Mushrooms
  • 🫒 Black olives
  • 🧅 Onion, 🍅 tomato
  • 🌽 Sweet corn
  • 🧂 Salt, pepper, Italian herbs
