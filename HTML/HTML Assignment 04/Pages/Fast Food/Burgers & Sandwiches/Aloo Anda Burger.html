🍔🥔🥚 Aloo Anda Burger

Description:
Aloo Anda Burger is a popular desi fast food treat, combining a crispy potato patty and a flavorful fried egg in a soft burger bun. Spiced with chutneys, fresh veggies, and tangy sauces, this burger delivers a unique fusion of taste and texture. Perfect for breakfast, lunch, or a quick snack on the go!

📝 Recipe Ingredients

  • 🥔 Potato patty (spiced and fried)
  • 🥚 Fried egg
  • 🍔 Burger bun
  • 🥗 Sliced onions, tomatoes, lettuce
  • 🌶️ Green chilies, coriander, mint
  • 🥣 Chutney or mayo-based sauce
  • 🧂 Salt, pepper, chaat masala
