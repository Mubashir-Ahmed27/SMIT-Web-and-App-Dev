🍔🔥 Zinger Burger

Description:
Zinger Burger is a crispy, spicy chicken burger loved for its crunchy coating and juicy fillet. Served in a soft bun with fresh lettuce, creamy mayo, and tangy sauces, it’s a fast food favorite for all ages. Perfect for lunch, dinner, or a quick snack when you crave bold flavor and satisfying crunch!

📝 Recipe Ingredients

  • 🍗 Crispy fried chicken fillet (spicy coating)
  • 🍔 Burger bun
  • 🥗 Lettuce, tomato, onion
  • 🥣 Mayonnaise, chili sauce
  • 🧂 Salt, pepper, paprika
  • 🥚 Egg (for coating)
  • 🌽 Cornflakes or breadcrumbs (for extra crunch)
