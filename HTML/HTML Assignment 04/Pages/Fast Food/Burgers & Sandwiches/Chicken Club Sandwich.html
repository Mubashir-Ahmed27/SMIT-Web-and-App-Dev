🥪🍗 Chicken Club Sandwich

Description:
Chicken Club Sandwich is a classic layered delight, featuring juicy grilled chicken, crispy lettuce, fresh tomatoes, and creamy mayo between toasted bread slices. This hearty sandwich is perfect for lunch, picnics, or a satisfying snack, offering a delicious balance of flavors and textures in every bite.

📝 Recipe Ingredients

  • 🍗 Grilled or shredded chicken
  • 🍞 Toasted bread slices
  • 🥗 Lettuce, 🍅 tomato, 🧅 onion
  • 🥓 Turkey bacon or regular bacon (optional)
  • 🥚 Fried egg (optional)
  • 🥣 Mayonnaise or sandwich spread
  • 🧂 Salt, pepper
