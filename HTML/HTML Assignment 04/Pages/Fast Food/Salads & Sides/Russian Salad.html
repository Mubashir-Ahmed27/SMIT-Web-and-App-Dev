🥗 Russian Salad

Description:
Russian Salad is a creamy, colorful delight made with boiled vegetables, fruits, and mayonnaise. This classic salad is a favorite at parties and buffets, offering a refreshing taste and rich texture. Enjoy it chilled as a side dish or a light meal for any occasion!

📝 Recipe Ingredients

  • 🥔 Boiled potatoes
  • 🥕 Boiled carrots
  • 🌽 Green peas
  • 🍏 Apple (diced)
  • 🍍 Pineapple (diced, optional)
  • 🥒 Cucumber (diced)
  • 🥚 Boiled eggs (optional)
  • 🥣 Mayonnaise, fresh cream
  • 🧂 Salt, pepper
