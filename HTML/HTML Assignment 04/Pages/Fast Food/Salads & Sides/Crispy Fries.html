🍟 Crispy Fries

Description:
Crispy Fries are a classic snack loved for their golden crunch and fluffy interior. Made from fresh potatoes, seasoned to perfection, and fried until irresistibly crisp, they’re the perfect side for burgers, sandwiches, or just on their own. Enjoy with ketchup, mayo, or your favorite dip!

📝 Recipe Ingredients

  • 🥔 Potatoes (peeled and cut into fries)
  • 🧂 Salt, pepper, paprika
  • 🛢️ Oil for frying
  • 🌿 Fresh parsley (optional, for garnish)
  • 🍅 Ketchup or mayo (for serving)
