🥗 Fresh Garden Salad

Description:
Fresh Garden Salad is a vibrant, healthy mix of crisp greens and colorful veggies, tossed in a light dressing. Perfect as a starter or side, it’s packed with vitamins and flavor, making every meal refreshing and nutritious. Enjoy this wholesome salad for a burst of freshness any time!

📝 Recipe Ingredients

  • 🥬 Lettuce or mixed greens
  • 🍅 Cherry tomatoes
  • 🥒 Cucumber
  • 🫑 Bell peppers (red, yellow, green)
  • 🧅 Red onion (optional)
  • 🥕 Carrot (shredded)
  • 🌽 Sweet corn (optional)
  • 🫒 Black olives (optional)
  • 🍋 Olive oil, lemon juice, salt, pepper (for dressing)
