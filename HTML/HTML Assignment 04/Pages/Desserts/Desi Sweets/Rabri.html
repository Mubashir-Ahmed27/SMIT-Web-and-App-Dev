🍮 Rabri

Description:
Rabri is a rich, creamy North Indian and Pakistani dessert made by simmering milk for hours until thickened, then sweetened and flavored with cardamom. Garnished with nuts and saffron, Rabri is served chilled and enjoyed at festivals and celebrations for its luxurious taste and texture.

📝 Recipe Ingredients

  • 🥛 Full-fat milk
  • 🍬 Sugar
  • 🌿 Cardamom powder
  • 🌰 Chopped nuts (almonds, pistachios)
  • 🧈 Ghee (optional)
  • 🧂 Saffron strands (optional)
