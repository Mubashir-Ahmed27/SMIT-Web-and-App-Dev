🍮 Gulab Jamun

Description:
Gulab Jamun is a beloved South Asian dessert, featuring soft, golden milk-based balls soaked in fragrant sugar syrup. Each bite melts in your mouth, offering a rich, sweet flavor with hints of cardamom and rose. Perfect for celebrations and festive occasions, Gulab Jamun is a true classic!

📝 Recipe Ingredients

  • 🥛 Khoya (milk solids) or milk powder
  • 🌾 All-purpose flour
  • 🧈 Ghee or butter
  • 🧂 Baking soda
  • 🍬 Sugar
  • 💧 Water
  • 🌹 Rose water or cardamom (for flavor)
  • 🛢️ Oil or ghee (for frying)
