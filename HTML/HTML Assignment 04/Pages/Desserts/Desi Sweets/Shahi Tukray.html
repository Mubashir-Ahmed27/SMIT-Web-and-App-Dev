🍞🍮 Shahi Tukray

Description:
Shahi Tukray is a royal Mughlai dessert, made with fried bread slices soaked in sweetened milk and flavored with cardamom and saffron. Garnished with nuts and silver leaf, this rich, creamy treat is a highlight of festive occasions and celebrations in South Asia.

📝 Recipe Ingredients

  • 🍞 Bread slices (fried or toasted)
  • 🥛 Full-fat milk
  • 🍬 Sugar
  • 🌿 Cardamom powder
  • 🧈 Ghee (for frying)
  • 🌰 Chopped nuts (almonds, pistachios)
  • 🧂 Saffron strands (optional)
  • 🥄 Silver leaf (optional, for garnish)
